How it works

Four steps from blank
file to shipped system.

The fastest path from brand identity to production-ready Figma library — no plugins-on-plugins required.

01

Set your brand identity

Input your brand colors — primary, secondary, and full semantic set. Each is checked for AA contrast, then pick your type, spacing, and radius character.

02

Auto-generate design tokens

Full 50–900 color ramps, a complete type scale, spacing, and radius — generated from your inputs as ~200+ Figma Variables in Light & Dark mode.

03

Choose your components

Tick the groups you need — Button, Input, Selection Controls, Badge, Feedback and more. Each shows exactly how many variants it ships.

04

Generate & ship to Figma

Review what's included, then generate. Everything lands as auto-layout-ready components across organized Figma pages — Pro unlocks Cards & Layout.

Ask us anything

Questions about Fluxa.

The most common questions before installing. Can't find what you're looking for? Drop us a line.

Is Fluxa affiliated with Figma? +
No. Fluxa is an independent Figma plugin built by Madhes. It's not endorsed by or sponsored by Figma.
Do I lose my system if I uninstall? +
No. Everything Fluxa generates lives natively in your Figma file as components and variables. The plugin is just the generator — uninstalling doesn't touch your work.
Can I customize the generated components? +
Absolutely. Every component is built with native Figma Auto Layout and Variables, so you can extend, restyle, or fork anything Fluxa creates.
Does the Free plan have a time limit? +
No. The Free plan is free forever — generate tokens, type, spacing, and up to 3 component groups as much as you like.
Does Fluxa support dark mode? +
Yes. Every component ships with light + dark mode variants wired up via Figma Variables modes. Toggle once, switch everywhere.
