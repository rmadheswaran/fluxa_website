We keep data collection minimal. Fluxa is a design tool, not a data business — here is exactly what we collect and why.

Last updated June 2, 2026 · Effective immediately

01The short version

Your design files stay yours. Fluxa runs generation locally in Figma. We do not read, store, or transmit the contents of your design files to our servers.

This policy explains what limited information we do collect when you use the plugin and website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

02Information we collect

Account data
Name and email when you create an account or subscribe.
Billing data
Handled by our payment processor; we store only plan status and the last four digits of your card.
Usage analytics
Anonymous, aggregated events (e.g. “a system was generated”) to improve the product.
Support messages
Content you send us when you ask for help.

We never collect the contents of your Figma files, the layers you create, or your design IP.

03How we use information

  • To provide and maintain the Service and your account.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To understand which features matter, using aggregated analytics.
  • To respond to support requests and important service notices.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your data to train models on your designs.

04Sharing & processors

We share data only with trusted service providers who help us operate — for example, payment processing, email delivery, and analytics. These providers are bound by contract to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

05Data retention

We keep account and billing data for as long as your account is active and as required to meet legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account data at any time.

06Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these, email us and we will respond within applicable timeframes.

07Security

We use industry-standard safeguards — encryption in transit, access controls, and least-privilege practices — to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure, but we work hard to keep your data safe.

08Children

Fluxa is intended for professional designers and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

09Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date above and, for material changes, provide additional notice. Please review periodically.
